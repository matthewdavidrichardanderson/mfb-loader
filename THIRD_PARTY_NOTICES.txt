MFB Loader links against the following third-party libraries:

libogc2
https://github.com/extremscorner/libogc2

This includes libogc, libdi, wiiuse, and lwBT. Their license texts and
notices are included in the licenses directory of binary distributions.

libfat
https://github.com/extremscorner/libfat

Its license text is included in the licenses directory of binary
distributions.

The physical-disc and apploader foundation follows TinyLoad by Hector Martin.
TinyLoad includes code from the Homebrew Channel stub, based on dhewg's Gecko
Loader stub, with copyright notices for Andre Heider and Hector Martin. TinyLoad
is licensed under version 2 of the GNU General Public License.

TinyLoad 0.2
https://wiibrew.org/wiki/TinyLoad

The game patch signatures and patch values were informed by the GPL-covered
implementations in WiiFlow Lite and Gecko OS:

WiiFlow Lite
https://github.com/Fledge68/WiiFlow_Lite

This includes the 480p pixel fix published by leseratte and based on the issue
identified by Extrems, as well as patch work originating in Gecko OS by Nuke.
